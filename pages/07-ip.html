Legal Agreement

Confidentiality & IP Rights

This is a legally binding agreement — not a policy document. By accepting your offer, you agreed to these terms. This module explains every clause clearly so you fully understand your obligations.

Why This Agreement Exists

Premas' most valuable asset is its knowledge.

Premas has invested over 20 years and tens of millions of rupees in building three proprietary scientific platforms, an IP portfolio of 10+ patents, and relationships with 150+ global clients. That investment lives in our data, our processes, our formulations, our client relationships, and our people's knowledge. The Confidentiality and IP Rights Agreement is the legal mechanism that protects it.

This agreement is not designed to restrict you unfairly — it is designed to ensure that the science and relationships built at Premas remain with Premas. The same protection applies in reverse: any IP you bring from a previous employer must be declared and remains yours or your previous employer's.

This agreement is binding during your employment and for a defined period after it ends. Read every clause. If you have any questions about your specific situation, speak to HR before taking any action that could constitute a breach.
Definitions

What the agreement covers — defined.

Def·1 What is "Company Information"? ▼

Company Information means all information disclosed to you — or that you come across — in the course of your employment. It includes:

Commercial Information:
  • Business strategies, plans, and financial position
  • Contract terms and negotiations with clients, partners, or suppliers
  • Pricing, revenue, and cost data
  • Client and partner identities and relationship details
Technical Information:
  • Trade secrets and know-how for any platform or product
  • Product specifications, processes, and formulations (D-CRYPT™, AXTEX-4D™, C-QWENCE™)
  • Technology, equipment design, and software
  • IP rights, patent applications, and unpublished research data
In any format: verbal, written, electronic, graphic, model, photograph, sample, computer disk, or any other form — whether or not labelled "confidential."
This covers information belonging to Premas Biotech and to all entities under the ultimate holding company (Zinchlor Technologies Ltd, registered in Guernsey).
Def·2 What is "Company Material"? ▼

Company Material means any physical or digital embodiment of Company Information — including documents, drawings, designs, models, photographs, samples, formulae, computer disks, files, or any other form of recorded Company Information.

This includes materials on company devices and on personal devices if they contain company data — laptops, phones, USB drives, cloud storage accounts.

Def·3 What are "IP Rights"? ▼

IP Rights means all forms of intellectual property, including:

  • Copyright (in written, visual, or software works)
  • Patent rights (including patentable inventions — even before a patent is filed)
  • Design rights (registered and unregistered)
  • Trade names, logos, and trademarks (registered and unregistered)
  • Moral rights and rights in get-up
  • The right to apply for registration of all of the above
Your 7 Obligations

What you have agreed to do — and not do.

Ob·1 Maintain Confidentiality — Always ▼
Treat all Company Information as strictly confidential at all times — during your employment and after it ends. There is no expiry on this obligation unless the information becomes publicly available through no act or omission of yours.
Ob·2 Return & Delete All Company Material on Request ▼
On request — at any time, including on the day you leave — you must deliver all Company Material in your possession, custody, or control to Premas, and permanently delete all Company Information from any device (company or personal) in your possession.
Ob·3 Limit Disclosure — Need-to-Know Only ▼
Disclose Company Information only to those who genuinely need to know it for the purposes of your employment — and only to those who are themselves bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations. This applies both internally and to external parties.
Ob·4 No Unauthorised Copying or Disclosure ▼
Do not copy or disclose any Company Information to any third party other than in the ordinary course of business and on a need-to-know basis — without the written consent of Premas. This includes screenshots, printouts, forwarded emails, and informal verbal conversations.
Ob·5 Use Company Information Only for Your Employment ▼
Do not use Company Information for any purpose other than your employment at Premas and activities authorised by the company. This means you cannot use Premas' proprietary knowledge, processes, or client relationships to benefit yourself, a third party, or any future employer.
Ob·6 Do Not Disclose Login Credentials ▼
You agree not to disclose any official credentials — including login information for any company application, system, device, or service — to any unauthorised third party, either during your employment or after its termination. Sharing passwords, access tokens, or system credentials is a breach of this agreement.
Ob·7 Do Not Disclose the Existence of This Agreement ▼
Without the company's written consent, you agree not to tell any third party that this agreement exists, or that Premas is disclosing Company Information to you. This applies during your employment and indefinitely thereafter.
Consequences of Breach: Failure to comply with these obligations may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination, legal action against you personally, and liability for any damages or losses incurred by Premas as a result of the breach. You also agree to indemnify Premas against all such losses.
IP Ownership

Everything you create here belongs to Premas.

This is one of the most important clauses in your agreement. In consideration of your remuneration and benefits, you agree that:

🏢

All IP Vests in the Company

Ownership of all IP Rights in any invention, idea, design, or original work that arises from your activities with Premas — whether embodied in materials or not — shall vest solely in Premas Biotech, for worldwide use, in perpetuity.

📝

IP Is Hereby Assigned

Such IP Rights and ownership are hereby assigned to Premas. You don't need to sign a separate assignment for each invention — the assignment is made at the time the IP is created.

✍️

You Must Sign What's Needed

Upon request and without further payment, you will sign all necessary documents and take any actions required to formally perfect Premas' title to the IP in India and any other country.

⏳

Survives for 1 Year After Exit

These IP ownership obligations continue for 1 year after your employment (or any consulting/advisory relationship with the Zinchlor Technologies group) ends.

Governing Law: The IP Rights Agreement is governed by Indian law. The exclusive jurisdiction for any disputes is the courts of Delhi.
Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation

Obligations that continue after you leave.

During your employment and for two years after your employment ends, you agree not to:

RestrictionWhat This Means in Practice
No Competing Business Do not be involved in any business that competes or is likely to compete with any part of Premas' business, or engage in the procurement or sale of products offered by Premas — anywhere in the world.
No Inducing Departures Do not persuade or attempt to persuade any Premas director or employee to leave their employment — directly or indirectly.
No Solicitation of Business Do not canvass or solicit orders for services similar to those provided by Premas from any Premas client or prospect — during or after employment.
No Disruption of Suppliers Do not induce or attempt to induce any Premas supplier to cease supplying, restrict supply, or vary their terms of supply to the company.
Why these restrictions are reasonable: These restrictions are explicitly acknowledged in your agreement as being reasonable and necessary for protecting the Company, Zinchlor Technologies, its nominees, associates, customers, suppliers, and business partners. They are governed by Indian law and enforceable in Delhi courts.
Export Controls

Technology transfer compliance.

As a Premas employee — especially in R&D, Manufacturing, or Regulatory — you may be involved in work that touches international technology transfer regulations. By signing your appointment, you confirm compliance with:

Indian Export Control Regulations Export Administration Regulations (EAR) International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) OFAC Regulations (US Treasury) Similar Foreign Laws
You confirm: That you have never been debarred, denied, embargoed, or sanctioned under any of these laws — as a person or as part of any entity. If this changes at any point during your employment, you must disclose it to HR immediately.
