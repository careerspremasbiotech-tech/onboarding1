Who We Are

A CDMO built at the frontier of biology.

Premas Biotech Private Limited was founded in 2005 at IMT Manesar, Gurugram, Haryana — at the heart of India's emerging biotech corridor. From a pioneering startup with a bold scientific vision, Premas has grown into one of India's most trusted biopharmaceutical Contract Development and Manufacturing Organisations (CDMOs).

The name says everything: Premas stands for "Production of Recombinant Molecules for the Advancement of Science." It is not a marketing slogan — it is a precise description of what we do every day. We partner with therapeutic developers across the globe to accelerate drug discovery — making programmes more efficient, more secure, and more certain.

What is a CDMO? A Contract Development and Manufacturing Organisation provides end-to-end biopharmaceutical services — from molecule scouting and cell line development, through process development and analytical assays, all the way to cGMP-grade manufacturing for clinical trials. Partners bring their molecules; Premas brings the expertise, equipment, and IP to develop and manufacture them.

Platform 01 · D-CRYPT™

Difficult proteins, delivered.

The majority of the world's most promising therapeutic targets are proteins that simply cannot be reliably expressed at scale using conventional systems — mammalian cells, E. coli, or standard yeast approaches consistently fail at the critical step of yield or quality.

D-CRYPT™ was built specifically for these molecules. It is a proprietary yeast-based expression platform that delivers high-yield production of Difficult-to-Express Proteins — singularly or in combination — where conventional systems fail. The platform represents over 15 years of original research into recombinant protein expression technology.

Why D-CRYPT™ Is Different

  • Proprietary yeast-based host system with unique expression machinery
  • Handles proteins that form inclusion bodies or are toxic in E. coli
  • Handles proteins that cannot be properly folded in mammalian systems
  • High-yield: consistent output suitable for clinical-scale production
  • cGMP-compatible from first batch — no re-development needed for scale-up
  • Over 500 proteins expressed on the platform to date

Key Programmes Supported

  • Oral Insulin (Phase 3) — late-stage clinical delivery milestone
  • Gates Foundation Rotavirus Vaccine — protein components delivered
  • Oravax Oral COVID-19 VLP Vaccine — VLP protein antigens at scale
  • Anti-Obesity Oral Drug (Phase 1) — cGMP protein for trial
  • 3 late-stage clinical programmes supported in total

Platform Spotlight · Oravax

An oral COVID-19 vaccine — made possible through D-CRYPT™.

Oravax is a Virus-Like Particle (VLP) triple-antigen oral vaccine candidate developed in partnership with Oramed Pharmaceuticals. VLPs mimic the structural architecture of SARS-CoV-2 without carrying any infectious genome — triggering an exceptionally strong immune response with a safety profile superior to conventional approaches.

The candidate integrates Premas' D-CRYPT™ expression platform — which produces the VLP protein antigens at scale — with Oramed's proprietary POD™ oral delivery technology. The result: an oral delivery of a complex multi-antigen VLP vaccine, which was once considered impossible, is now a clinical reality.

Platform 02 · AXTEX-4D™

Tumours that behave like the real thing.

One of the deepest failures in oncology drug development is the systematic unreliability of standard 2D cell culture models. Flat cell monolayers bear no structural or physiological resemblance to in-vivo tumours — resulting in drug candidates that look effective in the lab but fail in clinical trials.

AXTEX-4D™ generates three-dimensional tissueoids that authentically mirror the in-vivo tumour microenvironment. With contiguous cytoskeletal architecture, a genuine hypoxic core, and drug-resistance patterns that mirror clinical behaviour, AXTEX-4D™ makes preclinical data genuinely predictive.

🧫

3D Tissueoid Architecture

Contiguous cytoskeletal structure that replicates the spatial organisation of real tumour tissue — not a simple cell aggregate.

💨

Genuine Hypoxic Core

The oxygen gradient present in real tumours — and absent in standard cell culture — is replicated, enabling clinically relevant drug penetration studies.

🛡

Authentic Drug Resistance

Drug resistance patterns in AXTEX-4D™ models mirror those seen in clinical settings, dramatically improving the predictive power of preclinical screens.

Platform 03 · C-QWENCE™

India's genetic diversity, your competitive edge.

Antibody discovery is fundamentally a numbers game — the broader and more diverse your library, the greater the probability of finding high-affinity hits against difficult targets. Most commercial antibody libraries are built from Western donor populations, leaving the vast immunological diversity of South Asian genetics untapped.

C-QWENCE™ is a naïve human phage scFv antibody library built from India's unparalleled genetic heterogeneity — a population of 1.4 billion representing extraordinary immunological diversity. The result: over 10¹⁰ sequences, over 99% unique and patentable, with CDR profiles that routinely find high-affinity hits against targets where Western libraries have failed.
