Extended Hours

Business needs — programme deadlines, client visits, audits — may occasionally require working beyond standard hours. Premas values work-life balance and will seek to compensate extended hours with:

  • Compensatory off (Comp-Off) — most common
  • Time-in-lieu arrangement as per management discretion

Eligibility for comp-off varies by role and level. Check with HR for your specific entitlement.

Attendance System

Presence 360 — how attendance is tracked.

Premas uses the Presence 360 mobile application for all attendance and leave management. You will be set up on Presence 360 as part of your Day 1 onboarding. For any technical issues or access problems, contact the HR team directly.

📱

Daily Check-In / Check-Out

Log your daily attendance via the Presence 360 app. Ensure you mark both arrival and departure. Missing entries will be treated as absent for payroll purposes.

🌴

Leave Applications

All leave requests — EL, CL, SL — are applied and approved through Presence 360. Always apply in advance where possible; never assume leave is approved unless confirmed in the app.

📊

Leave Balances

Your real-time leave balance is visible in the app. Leave accrues on a pro-rata basis as specified. Unused EL can be carried forward subject to policy limits.

Attendance Cycle: The payroll attendance cycle runs from the 21st of the previous month to the 20th of the current month. Attendance data from this period feeds directly into payroll processing for that month.
Leave Entitlements

Your annual leave structure.

Premas provides a structured leave framework. All leave is managed through Presence 360. Below is your complete annual entitlement:

Leave TypeAnnual EntitlementKey Rules
Public Holidays 10 days per year List communicated at the start of each year. Fixed dates — cannot be substituted.
Earned Leave (EL) 18 days per year Cannot be availed during probation period. Accrues monthly. Carry-forward subject to policy. Requires advance notice and manager approval.
Casual Leave (CL) 7 days per year Cannot be clubbed with any other leave type. Maximum 2 days at any one time. Given on pro-rata basis from date of joining.
Sick Leave (SL) 7 days per year For medical reasons. A medical certificate may be required for consecutive sick leave exceeding 3 days. Given on pro-rata basis.
During Probation: Earned Leave (EL) cannot be availed during the 6-month probation period. Taking leave during probation is discouraged and may result in an extension of your probationary period. CL and SL may be availed on pro-rata basis for genuine need.
During Notice Period: EL, CL, and SL taken during a notice period do NOT count as days served in notice. Physical presence on the job is required for notice period completion. See Module 05 for full details.

What to Expect — Day 1

Your first day, prepared.

Morning
HR check-in and documentation

Report to HR. Submit all joining documents (see Module 08 checklist). Collect your access card, laptop, and any PPE relevant to your role.

Mid-Morning
HR induction session

Formal HR induction covering policies, attendance setup on Presence 360, IT setup, and introduction to the campus. Meet the HR team.

Afternoon
Department introduction

Meet your line manager, team, and key colleagues. Facility walkthrough. Introduction to your lab or workspace. Overview of your initial responsibilities.

First Week
Structured onboarding period

Safety training, SOPs relevant to your role, IT and system access, introduction to active programmes, and setting initial objectives with your manager. The first 30 days are formally a training period.
